This Demo focuses on Technical Art with a specialization in character rigging. I developed complete rigging systems for both the body and face, and created custom tools and plugins to optimize key rigging functionalities and streamline the workflow. The final animation results are achieved through retargeting in Unreal Engine or Faceware, demonstrating a full technical pipeline from motion capture data to final animated performance.
